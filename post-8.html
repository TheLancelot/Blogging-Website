Week 8

This week was all about completing my typeface including all the numbers. I had already completed the uppercase letters. In the first half of this week, I managed to complete the lower case letters and in the second half the numbers and punctuations. The whole process made me enjoy this week while I was designing them. I was finally feeling confident and proud that it was all coming together the way I wanted.
At the end of this week, both Vedang Sir and Lajja Maam took a look at what I had created and it finally got approved as one of the variation designs for the Britannia Typeface.

The feeling of achievement felt really good. More than one month of hard work paid off.

By the end of this week, I learned that whatever we put our minds into, the result will always be fruitful.
