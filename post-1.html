My First Week at Interbrand

Hello, welcome to my blog where I navigate through my journey of interning at the world's leading brand consultancy company. I am currently a 2nd-year visual communication student at École Intuit Lab and interning with one of the most well-known agencies from the past 40 years, Interbrand.

Before I go ahead with my work, I would like to tell you a bit about the agency and its approach to design. Interbrand has pioneered iconic work and forged many of the brand-building tools that are now commonplace. Their main aim is to forge iconic moves for brands through strategy and creativity. By turning customers into active participants. They help their clients strengthen their brands on an ongoing basis- their approach gives them the confidence to make iconic moves that spark desire and create unity, during extraordinary results.

I was going to be working under the guidance of the executive director Lajja Marjadi and creative director Vedang Shidore. I started my internship on June 13, 2022. On the day we commenced we had a quick introduction on how Interbrand works. We met with the entire team, I became extremely nervous and overwhelmed but everyone was extremely welcoming.

I got my first solo project as soon as the introduction was done. It was scary at first but with the opportunity of designing a typeface from scratch, I knew I was going to learn a lot in the next two months. The project was creating a Typeface for the “come alive” Britannia project. Interbrand in collaboration with Britannia industries has designed the identity and packaging for Britannia’s dairy portfolio to strengthen it. Vedang Sir gave tips every day on how to focus and make a font, taking as much as time needed to work on every single letter. The day and the following week was pretty much based on me exploring multiple variations of the letters that I was creating. Every letter had to be closely examined for its curves, edges, and nature. I also got to read the brand books on which Interbrand is mainly based. It was quite a lot to take in at once but at the same time, I was excited to start working at Interbrand.

Everyone made me feel as a part of their team at the very beginning due to which I felt pretty confident. Working with all senior professional designers and senior interns made me get the feel of what the real industry feels like.

Thank you! See you next week!
– Siddhi Sawant.
