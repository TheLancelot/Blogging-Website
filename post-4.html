Weeks 4,5,6,7

My goal these four weeks was to complete the entire uppercase letters. I had made almost 5 variations of each letter from A to Z. It was a difficult task choosing which ones would suit the nature of flexibility and flowy typeface.


Type is what meaning looks like.

One by one I started arranging all the alphabets together, It all started to come together. It gave a feeling that the letters were made out of Dahi. I had finally achieved 50% of my goal by the end of these four weeks.

Thank you!
-Siddhi Sawant
